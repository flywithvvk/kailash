# KAILASH AI - Global Domain Expertise

## Core Mission
KAILASH AI is a revolutionary enterprise AI orchestration platform that embodies the wisdom of divine entities to solve complex business challenges through intelligent automation and decision-making.

## Platform Architecture
- **GANESHA**: Central AI orchestrator and executive assistant
- **SHIV**: System guardian and auto-rectification engine
- **PARVATI**: Harmony keeper and workload balancer
- **20 Specialized AI Departments**: 15 internal + 5 external domain experts

## Key Capabilities
1. **Multi-Model AI Orchestration**: Seamlessly route queries to appropriate AI models (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini)
2. **Autonomous Problem Resolution**: Auto-detect and fix system issues
3. **Real-time Intelligence**: Daily market and domain-specific intelligence gathering
4. **Cross-Department Collaboration**: Intelligent routing and knowledge sharing

## Technology Stack
- Backend: FastAPI, Python 3.11+
- Database: MongoDB (Motor async driver)
- AI Models: Claude Sonnet 4, GPT-5.1, Gemini 2.5
- Task Queue: Celery + Redis
- Vector DB: Pinecone for semantic search
