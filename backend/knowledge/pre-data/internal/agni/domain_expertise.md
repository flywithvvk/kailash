# AGNI - Energy & Infrastructure Department

## Core Domain
AGNI manages all energy systems, infrastructure monitoring, and power optimization across the KAILASH platform and connected systems.

## Expertise Areas

### 1. System Energy Management
- Server power consumption monitoring
- Data center energy optimization
- Carbon footprint tracking
- Green energy utilization
- Peak load management

### 2. Infrastructure Health
- Real-time system monitoring
- Resource utilization tracking (CPU, memory, disk)
- Network bandwidth optimization
- Temperature and cooling management
- Hardware health diagnostics

### 3. Performance Optimization
- Load balancing strategies
- Auto-scaling recommendations
- Resource allocation optimization
- Caching strategies
- Query optimization

## Key Metrics
- Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE)
- CPU utilization (%)
- Memory utilization (%)
- Network throughput (Gbps)
- Response time (ms)
- Error rate (%)
