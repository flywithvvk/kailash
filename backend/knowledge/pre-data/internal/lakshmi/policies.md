# LAKSHMI Department - Financial Policies

## Revenue Recognition Policy

### Subscription Revenue
- Recognized ratably over the subscription period
- Deferred revenue for upfront annual payments
- Pro-rated refunds for early cancellations
- Revenue recorded in the currency of transaction

### Payment Processing
- All payments processed through certified payment gateways
- PCI-DSS compliance mandatory
- Customer card data never stored locally
- 3D Secure authentication for transactions > ₹10,000

## Refund Policy

### Eligibility
- Full refund within 7 days of purchase
- Pro-rated refund for subscription cancellation
- No refund for services already consumed
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days

### Process
1. User submits refund request
2. LAKSHMI validates eligibility
3. Approval required for amounts > ₹50,000
4. Refund initiated to original payment method
5. Confirmation email sent to user
6. Transaction logged in audit trail

## Expense Management Policy

### Approval Hierarchy
- < ₹10,000: Department head approval
- ₹10,000 - ₹1,00,000: CFO approval
- > ₹1,00,000: CEO approval
- Vendor payments: Finance team + CFO

### Expense Categories
1. **Cloud Infrastructure**: AWS, GCP, Azure costs
2. **AI Models**: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI API costs
3. **Human Resources**: Salaries, benefits, recruitment
4. **Marketing**: Advertising, content creation, events
5. **Operations**: Office, utilities, software licenses

### Cost Control Measures
- Monthly budget reviews
- Quarterly cost optimization sprints
- Automated alerts for budget overruns
- Unused resource identification and cleanup
- Vendor contract negotiations annually

## Financial Reporting Policy

### Internal Reports
- **Daily**: Cash position, transaction summary
- **Weekly**: Revenue trends, cost analysis
- **Monthly**: P&L, balance sheet, cash flow statement
- **Quarterly**: Board reports, investor updates

### External Reports
- Regulatory filings as per jurisdiction
- Investor reports (if applicable)
- Audit reports (annual)
- Tax returns (quarterly/annual)

### Data Accuracy
- All financial data reconciled daily
- Month-end close within 5 business days
- External audit annually
- Internal audit quarterly

## Fraud Prevention Policy

### Detection Mechanisms
- Real-time transaction monitoring
- Anomaly detection algorithms
- Velocity checks on transactions
- Geo-location verification
- Device fingerprinting

### Response Protocol
1. **Detection**: AI flags suspicious transaction
2. **Hold**: Transaction automatically held
3. **Investigation**: LAKSHMI analyzes pattern
4. **Verification**: User contacted for confirmation
5. **Action**: Approve, deny, or escalate to security
6. **Learning**: Update fraud models

### Zero-Tolerance Actions
- Chargebacks investigated immediately
- Fraudulent accounts permanently banned
- Law enforcement notified for criminal activity
- Payment gateway notified of fraud patterns
