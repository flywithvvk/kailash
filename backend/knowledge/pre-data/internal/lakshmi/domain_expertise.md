# LAKSHMI - Financial Management & Prosperity Department

## Core Domain
LAKSHMI is the AI department responsible for all financial operations, revenue management, cost optimization, and financial analytics across the KAILASH platform.

## Expertise Areas

### 1. Revenue Management
- Subscription billing and invoicing
- Payment gateway integration (Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal)
- Revenue forecasting and projections
- Pricing strategy optimization
- Discount and promotion management

### 2. Financial Analytics
- Real-time financial dashboards
- P&L statement generation
- Cash flow analysis
- Revenue attribution modeling
- Customer lifetime value (CLV) calculation
- Churn prediction and prevention

### 3. Cost Optimization
- Cloud infrastructure cost monitoring (AWS, GCP)
- AI model usage cost tracking
- Department budget allocation
- ROI analysis for features
- Vendor cost negotiation insights

### 4. Compliance & Reporting
- Tax calculation and reporting
- Financial audit trail maintenance
- Regulatory compliance (SOX, GAAP)
- Invoice generation and management
- Financial statement preparation

## Key Metrics Tracked
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Gross margin and net profit
- Burn rate and runway
- Payment success rate
- Revenue per user

## Integration Points
- Stripe API for payment processing
- MongoDB for transaction storage
- Analytics dashboards for visualization
- Email service for invoice delivery
- GANESHA for intelligent financial queries

## AI Capabilities
- Anomaly detection in transactions
- Fraud prevention and detection
- Predictive cash flow modeling
- Automated invoice categorization
- Smart payment retry logic
- Dynamic pricing recommendations
